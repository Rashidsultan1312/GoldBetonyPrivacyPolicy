Privacy Policy for Gold Betony

Last updated: April 06, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gold Betony handles user information.

Gold Betony is a football information app that provides standings, fixtures and match results using publicly available sports data from third-party sources.
How the App Works  
The app retrieves football data from third-party APIs and displays it directly on the user’s device.  
The app does not operate its own backend servers and does not store any user data.
Data Collection
Gold Betony does not collect, store or process any personal data.  
The app does not require account registration and does not request any personally identifiable information.
Local Storage
The app may store certain preferences locally on the user’s device, such as selected favourite teams.  
This data remains on the device and is not transmitted externally.
Tracking and Analytics
Gold Betony does not use tracking technologies, analytics tools or advertising frameworks.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version of the policy will be published together with the date of the latest update.

Continued use of the application after the publication of an updated version of the policy means that the user accepts its current version.
What the Application Does Not Do
The application:

- does not require account creation  
- does not include social features such as profiles, comments or chats  
- does not send marketing communications  
- does not sell or share user data for third-party marketing purposes
Children
Gold Betony is not specifically directed to children. It is intended for a broad audience interested in football, sports statistics, teams, matches, and analytical information.

We do not knowingly seek to collect personal data from children. If we become aware that such data has been provided without the required consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete it within the limits of our technical capabilities.
